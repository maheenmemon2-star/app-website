About SpotOn


What is SpotOn?

SpotOn is an app that lets you find the perfect hangout spot for you and your friends.
SpotOn lets you filter by criteria such as cost, dietary requirements, or physical needs so you can ensure that everyone has a good time with their needs met.
The days of spending hours finding a place to go are over with SpotOn instantly letting you find the perfect place to go out with your friends and family

What value does SpotOn create?

SpotOn helps drastically cut down on decision making time on hanging out with friends.
Being able to find a place to eat or hang out with family and friends while filtering by the groups needs saves lots of time in the decision making process. SpotOn allows you to find places to meet everyone's needs and is designed for group planning, as opposed to current alternatives which focus on more indiviudal perspectives and in no way solve the discord sown by trying to meet everybody's needs in the planning of group activities

Who is SpotOn for?

Although our target demographic is college students and young adults, SpotOn can be utilised by anyone looking to hang out with friends or trying to find a restaurant that fits the whole family's needs. SpotOn can be used by anyone and is made to cater to everyone.

How do we price SpotOn?

SpotOn creates revenue from advertisements within the app which are shown to users. Users can opt out of this by choosing to pay a small one time fee to remove ads. Businesses can also choose to subscribe to an visibility upgrade that shows their business more frequently. A profile improvement option is also available to businesses via a one time purchase
