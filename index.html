Find Your Spot

Who are SpotOn?

Hanging out with a group can be difficult. Everyone has different needs and preferences, this often makes it hard to find somewhere to go or something to do that suits everyone. And, that's where we come in. Here at SpotOn, we've worked hard to end the difficulties that come with hanging out. No more compromising, now you can find places and activities that fit the whole groups requirements and preferences. Now you can Find Your Spot.

Key Points About Us

Filter and Browse-

SpotOn lets you filter locations and activities to only show the ones that meet your groups requirements.
There are many different filters available such as dietary requirements, physical needs, or personal preferences.

Honest Descriptions and Reviews-

Other services that allow you to browse locations can often have misleading or inaccurate descriptions that are made by businesses witht the aim of making profit. We think that's unfair, so we've made sure that all of the business descriptions on SpotOn are fully accurate. We also prioritise and encourage users of SpotOn to give their own descriptions and reviews of places and activities, allowing you to see what people like you think.
