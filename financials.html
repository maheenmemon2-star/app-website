Revenue Streams

The app will generate revenue through three streams. These are advertisement based revenue, Premium subscriptions and business-profile cosmetics.

  1. Advertisements

    The app will contain advertisements placed at the bottom of the screen for companies who want to promote their business and the ads will be relevant to how the consumer uses the app for example, if the user frequently searches for sports activites, our algorithm will boost more ads about sports.

  2. Premium

    Businesses will have the option of of upgrading to a premium account which will make their businesses appear more frequently at the top of search results. The more they pay the higher their businees is displayed on search results lists.

  3. Profile Cosmetics

    A business can pay to upgrade their in-app profile to attract more customers and gain their trust. SpotOn will have a dedicated design team to this service.

Our Growth

  • App Launch

    This is the initial phase of our product's systematic growth. The App starts off with hundreds or thousands of users in the first 6 months only focusing on the county dublin region. Through promoting our app on TikTok and Instagram, we get more eyes on our app and the user base grows slowly and steadily.

  • Expansion

    SpotOn sees tens of thousands of users across Dublin alone and has expanded into other counties including the likes of Cork, Limerick and Galway with plans of expanding to the United Kingdom due to having no language barriers and will make integration easier.
