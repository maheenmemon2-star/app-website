Market Analysis

Overview

SpotOn is designed to solve a common problem among young people: choosing a place that suits everyone in a group. Instead of long group chats and indecision, the app provides quick, personalised suggestions based on shared preferences.

Target Audience

Age: 16–30, including secondary school students, college students, and young professionals.

Behaviour: Highly social, frequently organising group outings, relying on mobile apps and social media, and often making last-minute decisions.

Needs:

  • Quick group decision-making
  • Affordable options (student-friendly)
  • Dietary filters (vegan, halal, gluten-free)
  • New and interesting activities

Preferences: Fast, intuitive, mobile-first apps that are social and personalised.

Market Size & Growth

In Ireland, the 18–34 age group includes over 1.3 million people, representing a large and active digital audience. Research shows over 40% of young people took up new outdoor activities after COVID-19, highlighting increased interest in social experiences. Globally, platforms like Meetup and Eventbrite continue to grow, proving strong demand.

Competitor Analysis

Existing platforms such as Eventbrite, Yelp, and Google Maps provide useful features but focus mainly on individual users. They do not offer combined filtering for group needs or tools specifically designed for shared decision-making.

Why Users Will Choose Us

  • Designed specifically for group planning
  • Multiple filters applied at once (budget, dietary, activity)
  • Peer recommendations from other students
  • Focus on affordable and social experiences
  • Reduces time spent deciding and avoids conflict

Market Entry Challenges

Entering a competitive market is challenging due to strong brand loyalty towards apps like Google Maps and Yelp. Additionally, building a database of locations and reviews requires active users. These challenges will be addressed through targeted marketing and encouraging user-generated content.

Marketing Strategy

The app will be promoted through TikTok and Instagram, where young people spend significant time. Influencer marketing will be used to quickly build awareness, supported by viral content strategies and student-focused campaigns.

Sales Strategy

SpotOn will be distributed through the App Store and Google Play Store. This ensures trust, accessibility, and easy downloads while allowing for rapid growth and scalability.
