Market Analysis

Overview

Our app is designed for young people who regularly plan social outings. It simplifies group decision-making by helping users find places that suit everyone’s preferences quickly and easily.

Target Audience

Age: 16–30 (students and young adults)

Behaviour: Social, spontaneous, frequent group planners who rely on mobile apps.

Needs: Affordable options, dietary filters, and group-friendly decisions.

Preferences: Fast, mobile-first, social and personalised apps.

Market Size & Growth

Ireland has over 1.3 million people aged 18–34, forming a strong digital audience. Over 40% of young people took up new outdoor activities after COVID-19, showing growing demand for social discovery platforms.

Competitor Analysis

Platforms like Eventbrite, Yelp, and Google Maps offer partial solutions but focus on individuals. They lack group decision-making tools and combined filters for budget and dietary needs.

Why Users Will Choose Us

Our app is built specifically for groups. It combines multiple filters, encourages peer recommendations, and focuses on affordable social experiences—saving time and making sure everyone is happy.

Market Entry Challenges

Competing with established platforms and building a user base are key challenges. We will overcome this through student-focused marketing and encouraging user-generated content.

User Persona

College student who wants cheap places to eat with friends.
